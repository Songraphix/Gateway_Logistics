# Gateway Logistics & Services (SL) Ltd — Brand Guide
*Reference document for website development*

---

## 1. Color Palette
*Extracted directly from the company brochure*

| Swatch | Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 🟦 | **Navy (Primary)** | `#0A1F44` | 10, 31, 68 | Backgrounds, headers, footer, primary buttons, body text on light backgrounds |
| 🟨 | **Gold (Accent)** | `#D4AF37` | 212, 175, 55 | Highlighted headline words, dividers, icons accents, hover states |
| 🟦 | **Cyan / Sky Blue (Accent)** | `#0090D8` | 0, 144, 216 | Footer bar, icon circles, links, secondary CTAs, info highlights |
| ⬜ | **White** | `#FFFFFF` | 255, 255, 255 | Text on navy, page backgrounds, card backgrounds |
| ◻️ | **Light Gray** | `#F5F5F5` | 245, 245, 245 | Section backgrounds, alternating row backgrounds |
| ⬛ | **Body Text Gray** | `#333333` | 51, 51, 51 | Paragraph text on white backgrounds |
| ◽ | **Muted Gray** | `#666666` | 102, 102, 102 | Secondary text, captions, labels |

**Usage rule:** Navy is dominant (60%), white/light gray for breathing room (30%), gold + cyan as accents only (10% combined). Never use gold and cyan as backgrounds for large blocks — they're for highlights, icons, and small UI details only.

---

## 2. Typography

| Use | Font | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Headings | **Poppins** (Bold/SemiBold) | Matches the brochure's bold, modern sans-serif headline style |
| Body text | **Open Sans** (Regular/Medium) | Clean, highly readable for paragraphs and forms |

- Headlines use sentence case with a key word in gold (e.g. "Connecting Africa, **Delivering Excellence**")
- Avoid italics — the brand voice is direct and confident, not decorative

---

## 3. Logo Usage

- Primary logo: globe/network icon + "gateway" wordmark + "LOGISTICS & SERVICES (SL) LTD" subtext
- Use logo files as supplied by client (PNG/SVG) — do not recreate or recolor
- Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the globe icon
- On navy backgrounds, use the white/full-color version
- On white backgrounds, use the full-color version
- Never stretch, rotate, or recolor the logo

---

## 4. Iconography

- Style: **circular icons**, white linework on navy or cyan fill, consistent stroke weight
- Used for: core services, mining solutions, trust indicators (Reliable People, Safe Operations, On-Time Delivery, Trusted Partnerships)
- Keep icon style consistent across the entire site — outline style, not filled/solid icons, to match brochure

---

## 5. Imagery Style

- Real photography of trucks, heavy mining equipment, haulage trucks, and active job sites (as used in brochure)
- Images should feel authentic and operational — not generic stock photos
- Preferred treatment: subtle navy gradient overlay on hero images (as seen in brochure background) for text legibility
- Avoid overly bright or saturated filters — keep tones natural and professional

---

## 6. Brand Voice & Tone

- **Confident, direct, professional** — no fluff
- Speaks to decision-makers in mining, construction, oil & gas, and industrial sectors
- Key phrases already established by the brand (reuse across the site):
  - "Connecting Africa, Delivering Excellence"
  - "We move more than cargo. We move progress."
  - "Your success is our mission."
- Avoid overly casual language. This is a B2B brand serving serious industrial clients.

---

## 7. Motion & Interaction Style

- Subtle scroll-reveal animations on section entry (fade/slide up)
- Icon hover states can shift from navy to cyan or add a gold underline
- Avoid excessive or distracting motion — animation should feel premium, not playful
